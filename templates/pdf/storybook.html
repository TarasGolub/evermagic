The Night That Blinked

Charlie sits beneath the backyard sky, brown hair tucked under a backwards cap, light skin glowing in the moonlight. He studies his favourite T‑Rex toy when the stars seem to answer — a tiny starlight fossil drifts gently down to his feet. The world grows wonderfully quiet. An invitation to adventure shivers in the cool night air.

Rocket of Red and Roar

His treehouse folds itself into a comet-ship and Charlie climbs aboard, heartbeat quick with excitement. He tucks his T‑Rex plush beside the controls and remembers scoring three goals last Saturday — and the whole team cheering his name. The engine hums like a friendly drum. With a flash of silver light, they launch toward the stars.

The Fossil Field

An asteroid garden of glowing bone-tiles drifts before them — a gentle puzzle only a true dinosaur expert could solve. Charlie's brave heart steadies him. He remembers every T‑Rex bone by name, steps carefully across the wobbling fossil bridges, and realigns each piece. Every correct match hums with warm light.

The Constellation Roar

With the last bone set in place, the asteroids weave together into something magnificent — a towering star-dinosaur. A gentle cosmic T‑Rex unfurls across the sky, playful and grateful, its roar a ribbon of chiming stars. Charlie steers the comet-ship home through shimmering auroras as the galaxy applauds.

Dawn spills golden over the backyard as the comet-ship settles softly back into the treehouse. Charlie climbs down, still smelling like starlight.
