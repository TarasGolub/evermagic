## 2. Listing Structure

### 5 Separate Listings — One Per Theme

**Why not theme-as-variation:**
- A parent searching "princess storybook" who lands on a Space Hero listing and sees "Enchanted Princess" buried in a variation dropdown will leave
- Etsy cannot rank one listing well for 5 different theme keyword sets simultaneously
- Each listing needs its own photos, title, and tags optimized for that theme specifically
- 5 listings = 5x Etsy search surface area for virtually no extra cost ($0.20/listing)

**Launch order (by broadest search demand):**
1. Space Hero — broad "space adventure" keywords, strong for boys
2. Enchanted Princess — huge search volume on Etsy for princess + personalized
3. Fantasy Hero — covers "knight", "dragon", "adventure"
4. Animal Guardian — covers "animal lover", "nature", "pet"
5. Home Helper — more niche; launch last

## 4. Fulfillment Process (Post-Purchase)

### Customer Journey After Checkout

```
Customer purchases on Etsy
    ↓
Etsy sends order confirmation (automatically) containing:
    • "Thank you" message (customized by you — include Tally form link)
    • Digital download: "Welcome to EverMagic" PDF (one page, branded, with Tally link + instructions)
    ↓
Customer fills Tally form (3–5 min)
    ↓
n8n pipeline triggers automatically
    ↓
EverMagic generates book (automated)
    ↓
Delivery email sent to customer with PDF download links (~24–48h)
    ↓
You mark Etsy order as "complete"
```

### Etsy "Thank You" Message (auto-sent on purchase)
> Hi [name], thank you for your order! 🌟
>
> To create your personalized storybook, please complete our short personalization form here:
> **[TALLY FORM LINK]**
>
> It takes about 3 minutes. Your custom book will be delivered to your email within 24–48 hours of completing the form.
>
> Questions? Reply to this message — we're happy to help!

### Digital Download File
- File: a single-page branded PDF titled "Welcome to EverMagic — Next Steps"
- Contents: EverMagic logo, brief intro, Tally link as large clickable button, 3-step visual
- Purpose: backup delivery of the link in case customers miss the message; also required by Etsy to mark the order as a digital delivery

### If Form Not Submitted (48h later)
- Send a manual Etsy message: "Hi! Just checking in — did you receive the link for your personalization form? Happy to help if you have any trouble."
- Consider a simple n8n monitoring check: orders without a Tally submission 48h after estimated purchase time

## 5. Tally Form Branding Task

**Scope:** Visual only — no structural changes to avoid retesting intake flow.

**Plan:** Use free plan for launch (colors cover 80% of the brand). Upgrade to Pro after first few Etsy sales — logo + cover image are Pro-only but not launch-blockers.

### Free Plan Scope (confirmed)

Free plan covers: Theme, Font, Background, Text, Button background, Button text, Accent — that's it.
Everything under "Advanced" (inputs, buttons detail, logo, cover, custom CSS) requires **Tally Pro**.

Dark theme is not viable on free plan — placeholder colors can't be overridden, become unreadable.

### Settings to Apply — Light Theme

| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Theme | Light | readable, clean |
| Font | Inter | default, clean |
| Background | `#FDF8F2` | brand cream — subtle warmth |
| Text | `#37352F` | Tally default dark |
| Button background | `#F5A623` | gold — brand accent on every CTA |
| Button text | `#0B1628` | dark navy on gold |
| Accent | `#F5A623` | gold focus rings, highlights |

The form won't match the dramatic dark-navy PDF aesthetic, but gold buttons are the most customer-visible brand touch on a form. Clean, readable, professional.

### Pro Upgrade — after first consistent sales
Unlocks: logo, inputs styling, cover image, custom CSS — full dark-theme branding possible.
Break-even: ~2 Etsy orders/month at $14.99 CAD.

**Not changing:**
- Field names, field order, field types
- Validation logic
- Intake token mechanism

## 7. Marketing Strategy

### Phase 1 — Etsy Organic + Seeding Reviews (Weeks 1–4)

**Goal:** First 5–10 reviews to unlock Etsy's trust signals
**Tactics:**
- [ ] Research keywords with **eRank** (free tier) before publishing — validate title and tags against real search volume
- [ ] Start Etsy Ads at **$1–2/day** per listing — enough for impressions while organic builds
- [ ] Gift 3–5 free orders to UAT participants → ask them to purchase on Etsy (even at $1 with a coupon) and leave an honest review. Real purchase = real review = Etsy trusts it.
- [ ] Respond to every message within a few hours — feeds into Star Seller qualification
- [ ] Add listing to "free shipping" guarantee (digital = always free; make it explicit)

### Phase 2 — Pinterest (Weeks 2–6)

**Why:** Parents of young children are among the most active Pinterest demographics. Storybook content performs extremely well. Pins resurface organically for months.

- [ ] Create EverMagic Pinterest Business account
- [ ] Boards: "Personalized Kids Books", "Children's Gift Ideas", "Kids Adventure Stories", "Printable Activities for Kids"
- [ ] Pin each listing image directly (Etsy→Pinterest integration available)
- [ ] Create "story preview" pins: show a beautiful story page spread (use demo name "Emma" or "Lucas")
- [ ] Pin 5–7x per week for first 4 weeks — prioritize consistency over volume
- [ ] Use Pinterest's free analytics to see which themes drive most saves

### Phase 3 — Social & Influencer (Month 2+)

- [ ] **Instagram:** Post real book outputs (with parent permission). Format: carousel — cover → inside spread → coloring page → certificate. Caption: "Meet [Emma]'s Space Hero Mission 🚀"
- [ ] **TikTok:** "POV: you gave your kid a book where they're the hero" — simple screen recording of flipping through the PDF. High organic potential.
- [ ] **Facebook groups:** Parenting groups, personalized gift groups. Share genuinely — don't spam. "Made this for my daughter, happy to make one for your kid" style.
- [ ] **Micro-influencers:** Parenting bloggers, "gift ideas" accounts (5K–50K followers). Gift a free book in exchange for an honest post/reel. Target Canadian and US accounts.

### Phase 4 — Email List (Ongoing from Day 1)

- [ ] Add CTA in delivery email: "Loved the book? Share it with a friend and get 10% off your next order."
- [ ] **Tool:** Mailchimp free tier (up to 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month)
- [ ] **Send occasions:** New theme launches, Mother's Day, Father's Day, back to school, Christmas prep (October)
- [ ] Build list from: Etsy buyers (export emails from Etsy seller dashboard), Tally form opt-in field

## 9. Things You Might Forget

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Etsy Star Seller** | Requires: 95%+ on-time, 5-star avg, message reply rate. Build habits from day 1. |
| **Etsy fees total** | ~9.5% of sale price + $0.20 listing fee. Already priced in above. |
| **VAT on international sales** | Etsy collects and remits automatically for EU, UK, AU buyers. You do nothing. |
| **Canadian income tax** | Report Etsy income as business income on T1. Keep expense records (Tally Pro, Etsy Ads, etc.) |
| **Listing expiry** | Listings expire after 4 months. Enable auto-renew ($0.20 each time). |
| **Shop announcement** | Pin a message top of shop. Update seasonally. Start with trust builder. |
| **Returns policy** | Etsy can side with buyer in disputes. Document every order. Always offer re-generation before refund. |
| **Privacy** | Supabase PDF storage URLs should be time-limited or non-guessable. No child's data should be publicly indexed. |
| **Seasonal calendar** | Mother's Day (May 11), Father's Day (June 15), Back to School (August), Black Friday (Nov), Christmas (start Oct) |
| **Competitive research** | Search "personalized children's storybook" + "custom kids book pdf" on Etsy. Study top 5 sellers: photos, prices, review counts, titles. Do this BEFORE writing your own listings. |
| **Tally Pro check** | Custom CSS for Tally requires Pro plan. Verify before assuming it's free. |
| **Physical book path** | Lulu, Blurb, or Printify for print-on-demand when ready. Plan the workflow before offering. |
