| Theme | Launch with | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Fantasy Hero | **A** (Personalization) | Matches Space Hero format — consistent shop look |

| Animal Guardian | **C** (Emotion) | "Your Child Becomes" — strong emotional hook, less competition |
| Home Helper | **A** (Personalization) | Clearest value prop for a less obvious theme |
