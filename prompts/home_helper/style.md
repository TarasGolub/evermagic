# EverMagic — Home Helper Hero — Visual Style Guide

## Art Direction

All images follow a consistent **Pixar-style 3D animated** look with a **cozy warm home** palette:

- **Style:** High-quality 3D CGI render, Pixar animation style
- **Character proportions:** Exaggerated — large head, oversized expressive eyes, small body
- **Facial features:** Big round eyes, warm curious expression, soft cheeks — sense of wonder
- **Rendering:** Soft subsurface scattering, warm interior lighting, rich domestic textures
- **Lighting:** Warm amber home light with soft magical glow from the sprite. Every scene feels like the coziest room in the world.
- **Color palette:** Cozy home tones — warm amber (#f4a261, #e76f51), soft cream (#fefae0), sage green (#a7c957), wood brown (#b5793a). The most intimate and grounded palette in the catalogue.
- **Scale contrast:** The defining visual signature of this theme. The sprite is thumb-sized — always shown in clear scale contrast with real household objects (a spoon, a flower pot rim, a pencil). This contrast must be visible in every scene where the sprite appears.
- **Camera:** Often low-angle or close to ground level to show the tiny world's perspective. Child always appears large relative to the tiny world.
- **Mood:** Cozy, warm, secretly magical. Like discovering a miracle in the most familiar place in the world.

## Character Consistency Rules

Every image prompt MUST include:
1. Hair color + style (from order)
2. Skin tone (from order)
3. Outfit: exact description from `signature_look`
4. Glasses if `glasses: true`
5. Proportions: "Pixar-style proportions with oversized head and big expressive eyes"
6. Home sprite: size (thumb-sized), glow color, clothing, hobby-connected accessory, scale reference object — whenever present

> **Scale rule:** Whenever the sprite appears, specify a real household object for scale (e.g. "sprite standing beside a salt shaker", "sprite sitting on the rim of a flower pot"). This is non-negotiable for visual clarity.

## Cover Image Guidelines

- Hero kneeling with cupped hands, tiny glowing sprite visible in their palms
- Background: cozy home interior with tiny magical details (small doors in baseboards, glowing lights in flower pots)
- Lighting: warm amber with soft sprite glow
- Framing: the scale contrast between child and sprite is the hero of the image

## Coloring Page Guidelines

- Character with cupped hands, clean black line art on white
- Simple home background: flower pot, bookshelf, tiny doors in baseboard, stars, small glowing lights
- Include a tiny sprite beside a household object for scale
- Thick outlines suitable for children's coloring
