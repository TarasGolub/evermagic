# EverMagic — Enchanted Princess Adventure — Visual Style Guide

## Art Direction

All images follow a consistent **Pixar-style 3D animated** look with a **soft pastel magical** palette:

- **Style:** High-quality 3D CGI render, Pixar animation style
- **Character proportions:** Exaggerated — large head, oversized expressive eyes, small body
- **Facial features:** Big round eyes with light reflections, expressive eyebrows, soft round cheeks, warm smile
- **Rendering:** Soft subsurface scattering, smooth hair with soft shimmer, sparkle particle effects
- **Lighting:** Warm-soft magical light, sparkle-heavy. Every scene feels luminous and dream-like.
- **Color palette:** Soft pastel fantasy — lavender (#c9b3e8), rose (#f4a0b0), aqua (#a8dce8), soft gold (#ffe8a0), rainbow gradient accents. The most colorful and warm palette in the EverMagic catalogue.
- **Camera:** Cinematic compositions — emphasise the beauty of the world around the hero.
- **Mood:** Whimsical, warm, sparkly. Like a fairy tale with a strong "you are the magic" message.

## Character Consistency Rules

Every image prompt MUST include:
1. Hair color + style (from order)
2. Skin tone (from order)
3. Outfit: exact description from `signature_look`
4. Glasses if `glasses: true`
5. Proportions: "Pixar-style proportions with oversized head and big expressive eyes"
6. Unicorn companion: coat color, mane colors, size (pony-sized), horn — whenever present

## Cover Image Guidelines

- Hero beside unicorn companion, one hand resting on unicorn's mane, confident and warm
- Background: rainbow arch, glowing flower meadow, fairy castle in distance
- Lighting: luminous soft sparkle, pastel rainbow tones
- Framing: centered, warm, like a storybook cover

## Coloring Page Guidelines

- Character in graceful open-arm pose, clean black line art on white
- Simple fantasy background: unicorn, rainbow arch, castle towers, flowers, stars
- Thick outlines suitable for children's coloring
