# EverMagic — Animal Guardian Hero — Visual Style Guide

## Art Direction

All images follow a consistent **Pixar-style 3D animated** look with a **warm natural** palette:

- **Style:** High-quality 3D CGI render, Pixar animation style
- **Character proportions:** Exaggerated — large head, oversized expressive eyes, small body
- **Facial features:** Big round eyes, warm expression, soft cheeks — nurturing energy
- **Rendering:** Soft subsurface scattering, natural-feeling lighting, lush background detail
- **Lighting:** Warm natural golden light with a soft magical glow from the companion. Every scene feels alive and safe.
- **Color palette:** Rich nature tones — lush emerald (#2d6a4f, #40916c), warm gold (#f4a261, #ffd166), cool forest shadow (#1b4332), soft sage (#95d5b2). Warm and grounded — the most natural palette in the catalogue.
- **Camera:** Cinematic compositions — often at child eye-level or lower to create intimacy with the natural world.
- **Mood:** Warm, nurturing, quietly magical. Like a nature documentary with a gentle miracle at its heart.

## Character Consistency Rules

Every image prompt MUST include:
1. Hair color + style (from order)
2. Skin tone (from order)
3. Outfit: exact description from `signature_look`
4. Glasses if `glasses: true`
5. Proportions: "Pixar-style proportions with oversized head and big expressive eyes"
6. Magical animal companion: species, fur/feather color, glow color, markings — whenever present

## Cover Image Guidelines

- Hero kneeling gently, magical animal companion in cupped hands or beside them
- Background: magical forest clearing with glowing plants and golden light
- Lighting: warm golden natural light with soft magic glow
- Framing: centered, intimate — warm and safe

## Coloring Page Guidelines

- Character kneeling with arms open, clean black line art on white
- Simple nature background: forest trees, flowers, butterflies, woodland animals, winding path
- Thick outlines suitable for children's coloring
