# EverMagic — Fantasy Hero Quest — Visual Style Guide

## Art Direction

All images follow a consistent **Pixar-style 3D animated** look with an **enchanted fantasy** palette:

- **Style:** High-quality 3D CGI render, Pixar animation style
- **Character proportions:** Exaggerated — large head, oversized expressive eyes, small body
- **Facial features:** Big round eyes with light reflections, expressive eyebrows, soft round cheeks
- **Rendering:** Soft subsurface scattering on skin, smooth hair, subtle ambient occlusion
- **Lighting:** Cinematic, warm-magic key light with cool-forest fill. Every scene feels like a film frame.
- **Color palette:** Rich enchanted tones — deep forest greens (#1a3320, #2d5a3d), warm golds (#ffd166, #f4a261), cool magic blues (#4a90c4, #7bc4e2), and warm amber accents.
- **Camera:** Cinematic compositions — rule of thirds, depth of field, low angles to emphasise heroism.
- **Mood:** Epic fairy tale — grand but always warm and safe. Never dark or frightening.

## Character Consistency Rules

Every image prompt MUST include:
1. Hair color + style (from order)
2. Skin tone (from order)
3. Outfit: exact description from `signature_look`
4. Glasses if `glasses: true`
5. Proportions: "Pixar-style proportions with oversized head and big expressive eyes"
6. Dragon companion: color, size (dog-sized), markings — whenever present in scene

## Cover Image Guidelines

- Hero and dragon in a **powerful, confident pose** together
- Background: enchanted kingdom with glowing castle spires and twilight sky
- Lighting: dramatic rim light, warm key light on face
- Framing: centered, low angle, movie poster feel

## Coloring Page Guidelines

- Same character in heroic pose, clean black line art on white
- Simple fantasy background: enchanted trees, castle tower, small dragon outline, stars
- Thick outlines suitable for children's coloring
